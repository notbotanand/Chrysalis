# Acme Payments (FICTIONAL EXAMPLE)

> Fictional company. See `examples/README.md`.

## Business Overview
Acme Payments is a developer-first payments platform for B2B SaaS
companies. Founded 2018, Series C ($120M raised, $1.2B valuation as of
late 2025). The core product is a Stripe-alternative focused specifically
on usage-based billing — metering, invoicing, dunning, revenue recognition
for SaaS companies whose pricing depends on consumption. ([Acme Series C
announcement, Oct 2025](https://example.com/acme-series-c))

Approximately 280 employees, HQ in San Francisco with engineering hubs
in Toronto and Dublin. Revenue not publicly disclosed; reported $40M ARR
in mid-2025 per industry coverage. ([SaaStr profile, Aug 2025](https://example.com/saastr-acme))

## User Base & Geography
Primary users are developers and revenue-operations leaders at mid-market
SaaS companies ($10M–$200M ARR). Approximately 800 paying customers as of
Q4 2025. ~70% North America, 20% Europe, 10% rest of world. Heavy in
developer-tools, observability, and AI-infrastructure sub-segments —
companies that price per API call, per GB, or per active user.

End-users (the people who actually interact with the product daily) are
backend engineers integrating the metering SDK, and finance/revops
analysts reading the invoicing dashboards.
